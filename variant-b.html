1

Nothing goes external without approval

Emails, messages, deployments, and API calls to external services are gated. The fleet proposes; the human decides.

2

Every action is signed and traceable

Who asked, which agent executed, under what authority, and when. A complete chain of custody for every operation.

3

Reversible first

When uncertain, prefer the action that can be undone. Destructive operations require additional confirmation gates.
